## About
This portfolio serves as a hub for displaying my journey as a developer, presenting my skills in **HTML**, **CSS**, **Bootstrap**, and **SASS**. Additionally, it features a collection of projects I have worked on, a downloadable resume, and a contact form for direct inquiries.

## Features
- **Responsive Design** using Bootstrap for all screen sizes.
- **Dynamic Sections** including:
  - Home
  - About Me
  - Projects
  - Contact Form
- **Interactive Elements** such as a navbar, email button, and project showcase.
- **Logos of Technologies** used in my development journey.
- Downloadable resume option.

## Technologies Used
- **HTML5**
- **CSS3**
- **Bootstrap 5.3**
- **SASS**
- **Google Fonts (DM Sans)**
